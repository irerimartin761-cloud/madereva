About Madereva.com

Madereva.com is a digital platform designed to connect professional, trained chauffeurs with clients requiring transportation services. The platform acts as a secure intermediary for communication, bookings, and protected information exchange.

Purpose — For Drivers

  • Showcase skills, qualifications and vehicles
  • Be selected for chauffeur, logistics, escort and private driving tasks
  • Access training, certification and career growth

Purpose — For Clients

  • Hire vetted, trained drivers quickly
  • Compare qualifications, ratings and availability
  • Book secure, reliable transport for events, travel and daily needs

Driver Benefits & Support

Madereva supports drivers with training (defensive driving, VIP service), equipment (dashcams, phone holders, power banks), subsidized insurance packages, feedback & evaluation programs, branding and incentives.
